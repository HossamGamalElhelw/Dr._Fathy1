Dr. Fathy Mohamed is a highly respected professor and controller in the Department of Electronic and Communication Engineering at Beni-Seuf University. He is an expert in his field known for exceptional teaching skills, research contributions and dedication to his students.

Bio

He is a highly respected and accomplished professor in the Department of Electronic and Communication Engineering at Beni-Seuf University.
He has been teaching a wide range of courses to undergraduate and graduate students for many years, and is considered to be an expert in his field.
He has received awards and honors for his work and is a member of professional organizations He is committed to staying current in his field.
He has been an active researcher in the field of electronic and communication engineering and has published numerous papers and presented his research at conferences around the world.
